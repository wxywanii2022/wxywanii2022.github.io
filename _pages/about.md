👋 **Welcome to my homepage!**

I am a first-year **MSCS student** at **Stanford University**. Prior to Stanford, I earned my **B.S. in Mathematics & Computer Science** with a **minor in Statistics** from the **University of Illinois Urbana–Champaign** (2023–2026). I graduated with multiple honors, including [Highest Distinction in Mathematics & Computer Science](https://catalog.illinois.edu/undergraduate/eng_las/mathematics-computer-science-bslas/#degreerequirementstext), [Magna Cum Laude](https://las.illinois.edu/academics/distinctions), and [Bronze Tablet Scholar](https://siebelschool.illinois.edu/about/awards/undergraduate-scholarships-awards/bronze-tablet-award), one of the University's highest undergraduate academic honors.

I am broadly interested in **foundation models, AI reasoning, multimodal learning, and scientific machine learning**. My research explores how learning, reasoning, and external knowledge can be combined to build more capable, reliable, and impactful AI systems for solving complex real-world problems.
